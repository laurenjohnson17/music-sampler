Robert Francis: The love child of folk and indie music

A formal introduction to Robert Francis

I first listened to Robert Francis after receiving his song ‘Junebug’ for free through iTunes, and I was instantly hooked. Francis, a California native, debuted his first album ‘One by One’ in 2007, and has since published 3 other albums. I would describe Francis as an indie-blues singer with a touch of folk influences. Robert’s songs convey strong ties to the human experience through emotional lyrics. Anyone can relate on an emotional level to a Robert Francis song. Robert next album is named ‘Empire Blues’, and is set to be released this year.
